Graphical Interface of Structure in Knowledge

(pronounced gee-sig, 지식, 知识) Lab at the Northern Illinois University.

In our laboratory, we conduct research to examine how the visualization of knowledge structure can support teachers’ practice and scaffold students’ learning, thereby supporting the design of instructional strategies that target individual learning problems in deriving better outcomes.
